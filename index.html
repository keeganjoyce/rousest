a bit of background

Keegan Joyce was born in 1989 in Sydney, Australia. As a child he wanted to be an "Astronaut/President" (he still believes it will be a thing), but when the opportunity came to star in Cameron Mackintosh's Australasian tour of 'Oliver!' there was no turning back. After his professional debut on stage, Keegan finished his schooling whilst pursuing a career in the performance industry. Keegan has worked on stage, in film and television and most recently has enjoyed success in three award-winning series of Rake for the ABC. In 2014 Keegan finished his degree in Musicology at The Sydney Conservatorium of Music and also played Arnold in the second season of Please Like Me for ABC2 and Pivot TV. Keegan returned to the stage in 2014 with Once: A new Musical, playing the bass, ukulele, and guitar as the character Andrej. Keegan has worked with some of Australia's most celebrated performers and industry professionals. He has a wide range of musical skills and recently sang Tenor for a Gregorian Chant album produced by the ABC, a particular musicological interest of his.

To contact Keegan please speak to his representatives at Marquee Management.

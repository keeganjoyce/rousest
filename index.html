Rouse St.

relax in luxury

Rouse St. Port Melbourne is only 10 minutes from Melbourne's CBD, but feels worlds away. Relax in the luxuriously appointed 2 bedroom industrial town-house nestled within the former Swallow & Ariel Biscuit Manufactory. Wake up your senses with the fresh fragrance of Melbourne's Port Phillip bay, and take a short walk to wonderful coffee & cafés, gourmet produce & restaurants, and stroll the sunny beach front. Only one block away from the beach front, take a dip in the refreshing bay waters or watch the Spirit of Tasmania float off into the sunset. Come forget your troubles and make yourself at home.
